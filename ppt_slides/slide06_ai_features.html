AI Capabilities: Not Just Summaries

1. Personalized Strategy Matching

Analyzes user's token holdings and risk profile to give targeted recommendations.

"You hold significant ETH. This proposal increases staking rewards → 85% match with your Conservative Growth strategy"

2. Risk Assessment

4-tier risk grading (Low/Medium/High/Critical) with technical justification.

"Risk: HIGH - Contract upgrade with unaudited code, $5M treasury impact"

3. Security Scanning

Automated checks for contract addresses, treasury impact, governance attacks.

"✓ Contract verified: Aave V3 Treasury | ⚠️ $2M token transfer requested"

4. Conversational Research

Users can ask follow-up questions about any proposal in natural language.

Q: "How does this affect Aave?"

A: "This increases reserve factor by 2%, boosting protocol revenue..."
